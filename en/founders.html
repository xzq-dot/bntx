Founders Split Off: Back to Next-Gen mRNA

Announced March 2026 — the two co-founders will leave BioNTech to build an independent company focused on the frontier of mRNA. A "split but not a break-up," and also one of the market's concerns about BNTX.

05 What It Means for Investors

⚠ A double-edged sword

Upside: the founders return to the frontier they excel at, while core BioNTech can focus single-mindedly on commercializing late-stage assets like BNT327 — a cleaner division of labor. Concern: the departure of the driving figures from day-to-day operations is a clear governance and execution risk for BNTX — a factor cited when analysts trimmed price targets. Also note: the official framing is "next-generation mRNA platform," not AI as a core direction — some online interpretations need scrutiny.
